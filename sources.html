About ROCK PAPER SCISSORS

ROCK PAPER SCISSORS is a project for my Advanced Web Design course that demonstrates the use of web fonts and CSS animation.

The animation demonstrate the relationship between rock, paper, and scissors.
The words were included in the SVGs wherever they could to further develop to conflict between them.

Falcon font was chosen for the scissors font as this shows sharp edges, and has cuts throughout the font.
Science Gothic is a variable font used for the rock. It has a strong and sturdy foundation, something as strong as a rock.
Skia font was chosen for the paper. This was chosen in Inkscape and exported as an SVG.
